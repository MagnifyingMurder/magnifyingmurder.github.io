The Fatal Shooting of Tupac Shakur

On September 7, 1996, Tupac Shakur was shot 4 times in a drive-by shooting in Las Vegas, Nevada. He was hit two times in the chest, one in the arm and one in the thigh. Tupac was then rushed to the University Medical Center of Southern Nevada.

September 7 1996

Death of Tupac

Shakur passed away of respiratory failure that led to cardiac arrest after the removeal of his right lung.

September 13 1996

Cuba

In 2004, a man looking like Tupac appeared in a video in which he was joking around with Suge Knight. Suge Knight was with Tupac the night he was shot, leading many to think he faked his death.

2004

New York

A man, seemingly Tupac, in a yellow mac was seen in the Occupy Wall Street demo. Many of the rapper's fan have pointed that the Tupac always wanted to get involved in politics. His mother Afeni even spoke out at the time to praise the thousands who took part in the protest.

2011

Sweden

Unknown Location
